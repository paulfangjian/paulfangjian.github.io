Recommended Books and Papers

The following are some useful books recommended by my advisor, professors and myself for CS/EE students.
You can download electronic edition of some of them for free by clicking the book title. But I highly recommend you buy hard-copies, since they will bring you totally different feelings. I will also provide some taobao shops where you can buy cheap copy editions if you can not afford expensive English original books (not recommended, we should support genuine!)

English Writing:
The Elements of Style: A tiny book about English writing for native speakers of English.
Science Research Writing: A guide for non-native speakers of English.
Writing for Computer Science
How to Do Research:
Crafting Your Research Future: A guide to successful Master’s and PHD degrees in Science and Engineering.
How to Write a Great Research Paper (Video): This slides offers seven simple, concrete suggestions for how to improve your research papers.
How to Write a Great Research Proposal: This page is an attempt to collect together a number of suggestions about what makes a good proposal.
How to Give a Great Research Talk (Video): This slides describe simple guidelines for giving talks.
